WHYUS
Living in New York City, it has become apparent that the majority of its inhabitants interact with the digital world on a daily basis. From checking the latest news headlines to shopping online for groceries, our lives have become increasingly intertwined with the internet. As a result, the importance of websites has grown significantly, with businesses relying on them to attract and retain customers.

However, it is disheartening to see that many commercial and small business websites in New York City are outdated. Despite the fast-paced and dynamic nature of the city, these websites fail to keep up with the times, potentially hindering their success. A poorly designed website can be a major turnoff for visitors, leading them to seek out other options instead.
After residing in New York City for a period of nine years, it has become apparent that the average New Yorker visits at least four websites on a daily basis. However, it has also been observed that a significant number of commercial website and small business websites are outdated. Given that New York City is one of the fastest-paced and most populous cities in the world, digital interaction has become an integral part of our daily routines. It is crucial for each website to make a positive impression on visitors and convert them into customers, as they are presented with only one opportunity to do so. In this regard, United of web undertake a comprehensive analysis and conduct research on human behavior in the context of the web.


It is crucial for businesses to realize that a website is more than just an online presence. It is an opportunity to make a positive impression on potential customers and to showcase their products and services in the best possible light. A well-designed website can enhance a business's credibility, making it more attractive to customers who are seeking reliable and trustworthy vendors.

In order to create a website that can make a lasting impact on visitors, it is essential to take into account human behavior and how people interact with the web. By understanding what drives people to visit and engage with certain websites, businesses can tailor their websites to appeal to their target audience.

One of the most important considerations is the user experience (UX) of the website. UX refers to the overall experience a user has when navigating a website, encompassing elements such as ease of use, accessibility, and visual appeal. In order to create a positive UX, it is essential to understand the needs and preferences of the target audience.

For example, if a website caters to an older demographic, it may be necessary to increase the font size and use contrasting colors to improve readability. On the other hand, if the website is geared towards a younger audience, incorporating visually appealing graphics and animation may be more effective in capturing their attention.

Another important consideration is the website's functionality. A website should be easy to navigate, with intuitive menus and clear calls to action. Visitors should be able to quickly find the information they are looking for and complete any necessary actions (such as making a purchase) without encountering any obstacles.

In addition, the website's loading speed is also critical. Visitors are more likely to abandon a website if it takes too long to load, so optimizing the website's performance is essential. This can include compressing images and minimizing code, among other techniques.

Lastly, businesses must also take into account the mobile experience. With the majority of internet usage now taking place on mobile devices, it is essential for websites to be optimized for mobile screens. This can include using responsive design, which adjusts the website's layout and functionality based on the screen size of the device being used.

In conclusion, in a city like New York where digital interaction has become a routine part of our lives, businesses must take website design seriously. A well-designed website can make a significant difference in attracting and retaining customers, while a poorly designed website can have the opposite effect. By understanding human behavior and incorporating the latest design trends and technologies, businesses can create websites that make a lasting impact on visitors and enhance their overall success.
